Sreehari is a trusted career / talent advisor for senior candidates and a variety of clients (Indian Conglomerates, Blue-chip MNCs, Indian Owner driven organisations and mid-sized firms) across industrial and consumer segments for the past 20 years.

He has personally led successful domestic search engagements in multiple industry sectors and functional disciplines. Client and candidate relationships built over the years coupled with a deep understanding of the Indian corporate landscape and talent marketplace makes Sreehari an effective and incisive consultant with a strong track record of delivering successfully on difficult and complex searches. Before his career in executive search Sreehari worked in Brooke Bond, Wipro Infrastructure Engineering and ITW Signode, handling business roles.

As a co-founder of ACCRETE, Sreehari personally leads leadership hiring across sectors with special focus on the Industrial and Consumer Segments and a range of functions including managing director, CEO / COO, functional heads in engineering, sales, marketing and operations. He has extensive experience in domestic recruitment, having assisted several “start-up” operations. Before co-founding ACCRETE, Sreehari worked with ABC Consultants, Stanton Chase International and RGF Executive Search.

Sreehari holds a bachelor’s degree in Mechanical Engineering.
